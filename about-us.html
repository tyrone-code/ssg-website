Our History

Schoeman Sejwane Grobler Attorneys opened its doors in 1998 with the goal of providing comprehensive tailored legal services to individuals and business owners alike.

Being a smaller firm, we have the ability to provide a tailored service by establishing personal relationships with all of our clients. We pride ourselves on excellent service, ensuring that our clients are always brought up to date with the status of their matters as and when new developments arise.

Our firm is based on principles of honesty, integrity and putting our clients’ needs first.

To find out how we can help you, please contact us for a free no obligation consultation. We would love to meet you to discuss your requirements.
