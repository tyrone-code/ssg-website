About US

Find out More about us



Our mission is to strive to add significant long-term value to our clients and their stakeholders, through the delivery of our services.


Law has an extremely wide scope, and it is impossible to be a proper “jack of all trades” SSG Attorneys is a boutique law firm offering legal services in select and niche practice areas. This is as a direct result of previous work experience and careers by our director and staff compliment. Both our professional and key support staff were previously employed at various trust companies as a result of which the firm’s core offering is in the fiduciary services industry (Estate planning, Trustees, Executors). In addition, our services are complemented by extensive experience in the Insolvency Industry, and we render services in the financial distressed sector, advise on liquidation, sequestration, debt review, rehabilitation & related matters.
